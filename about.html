About
Me

My name is Mara Turner and I am 18 years old.

I am a self-taught artist with low needs autism. As an artist, I find that creating is the best way for me to express how I see the world, focusing on fine details and unique perspectives.

My Artistic Journey

I've been working with graphite since 4th grade. In recent years I’ve been experimenting with acrylic paint and oil paint which I have come to love.

What I Create

  • Traditional Art: I love working with graphite, charcoal, and paints to create physical pieces.
  • Alternative Style: My work often leans into gothic, alternative, and expressive aesthetics.
